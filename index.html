What We Do

We offer rental, service and sell all sorts of freeride equipment. Everything from fat skis to avalaunch backpacks to telemarks. Welcome to Engelbergs most experienced ski shop.

Rental

We rent all kinds of skis: Freeride, Touring, All Mountain and Pist Skis.
Boots: Piste and Freeride (walking mode)
Avalanche Equipment: Tranciever, Shovel, Probe and Avalanche Back Pack.
